# Cancellation & Refund Policy

## 24-Hour Risk-Free Cancellation

Any booking made through Wanderlust Travel can be cancelled free of charge within 24 hours of purchase, provided the departure date is at least 7 days away. A full refund is issued to the original payment method within 5-7 business days. This applies to all fare types, including Basic Economy and other non-refundable fares.

## Standard Cancellations (Refundable Fares)

Refundable fares (Flex, Business, and First Class tickets) can be cancelled at any time before departure for a full refund, minus a $25 per-passenger processing fee. Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 7-10 business days.

## Non-Refundable Fares

Basic Economy and Standard Economy fares are non-refundable after the 24-hour window. Cancelling a non-refundable ticket converts the value (minus a $150 per-passenger cancellation fee) into a travel credit valid for 12 months from the original purchase date. Travel credits cannot be redeemed for cash and are non-transferable.

## Airline-Initiated Cancellations & Schedule Changes

If the airline cancels a flight or changes the scheduled departure time by more than 90 minutes, affected passengers are entitled to a full refund to the original payment method regardless of fare type, or free rebooking on the next available flight. No cancellation or change fees apply in this case.

## Cancellations Due to Medical or Family Emergencies

Passengers who cancel due to a documented medical emergency, hospitalization, or death in the immediate family may request a fee waiver on non-refundable fares. Supporting documentation (e.g., a doctor's note or death certificate) must be submitted within 14 days of the cancellation. Approved waivers convert the ticket to a travel credit with no cancellation fee; cash refunds are not issued under this policy unless the fare was already refundable.

## How to Cancel

Cancellations can be made online through the "Manage Booking" page using the booking reference and last name, or by contacting support. Partial cancellations (cancelling one passenger on a multi-passenger booking) are supported for refundable fares but not for non-refundable group bookings, which must be cancelled in full.
