# Travel Insurance Policy

Wanderlust Travel offers an optional Travel Protection Plan that can be added at checkout for a fee starting at 6-8% of the total trip cost, depending on traveler age and trip length.

## What's Covered

The standard plan covers trip cancellation, trip interruption, emergency medical care, emergency evacuation, baggage loss/delay, and travel delay. Add-on coverage is available for "cancel for any reason" (CFAR), rental car damage, and adventure-sports activities.

## Trip Cancellation & Interruption

If a covered traveler must cancel or cut a trip short due to a covered reason (e.g., sudden illness or injury, death of a family member, severe weather, or involuntary job loss), the plan reimburses 100% of prepaid, non-refundable trip costs (cancellation) or unused trip costs plus reasonable additional transportation expenses to return home (interruption). Claims must be filed within 90 days of the trip's scheduled return date, with supporting documentation (medical records, death certificate, employer letter, etc.).

The optional "Cancel For Any Reason" (CFAR) add-on reimburses 75% of prepaid trip costs for cancellations not otherwise covered, provided the trip is cancelled at least 48 hours before departure and the plan was purchased within 14 days of the initial trip deposit.

## Medical & Emergency Evacuation

Emergency medical expenses incurred while traveling are covered up to $50,000 per person for medical treatment and up to $500,000 per person for emergency medical evacuation/repatriation. Coverage applies to sudden illness or injury during the trip; pre-existing conditions are covered only if the plan was purchased within 14 days of the initial trip deposit and the traveler was medically able to travel at time of purchase.

## Baggage Protection

Checked or carry-on baggage that is lost, stolen, or damaged during the covered trip is reimbursed up to $2,500 per person, subject to per-item sub-limits (e.g., $500 for a single electronic device). This coverage applies in addition to, and after, any compensation received directly from the airline for the same loss.

## Travel Delay

If a covered trip is delayed more than 6 hours due to a covered reason (weather, mechanical issues, common carrier delay), reasonable additional expenses such as meals and lodging are reimbursed up to $200/day, capped at $1,000 per person per trip.

## How to File a Claim

Claims can be filed online through the insurance portal linked in the booking confirmation email, or by contacting the insurance provider's claims line directly. Required documentation typically includes the original booking confirmation, proof of the covered event (medical note, airline delay confirmation, police report, etc.), and receipts for any reimbursable expenses. Most claims are processed within 10-15 business days of receiving complete documentation.

## Exclusions

The plan does not cover: cancellations due to a traveler's own change of mind (unless CFAR was purchased), pre-existing conditions not covered under the look-back waiver, injuries sustained during excluded high-risk activities (unless the adventure-sports add-on was purchased), acts of war, and losses resulting from failure to obtain required travel documents (passport, visa) in advance.
